# Trademark Guidelines

This document outlines how you may and may not use the Desygn AI name and logo.

## Name Usage

**"Desygn AI"** is the official name of this project and is protected as a trademark.

### Acceptable Uses

- "Built with Desygn AI"
- "Powered by Desygn AI"
- "Compatible with Desygn AI"
- Referring to the project by name in articles, blog posts, or documentation
- Describing your product's integration with or support for Desygn AI

### Not Acceptable Uses

- Using the name in a way that implies official endorsement by the Desygn AI maintainers
- Using the name in a competing or substantially similar product's branding
- Registering domain names, social media handles, or company names that include "Desygn AI" without permission
- Modifying the name (e.g., "DesygnAI Pro", "Desygn AI Plus") to suggest an official variant

## Logo Usage

The Desygn AI logo may be used under the following conditions:

- **Linking**: You may use the logo to link to the official Desygn AI repository or website.
- **Attribution**: You may include the logo in "powered by" or "built with" badges on your own projects.

### Logo Restrictions

- Do not modify, distort, recolor, or alter the logo in any way.
- Do not use the logo in a manner that suggests sponsorship, partnership, or official affiliation unless you have explicit written permission.
- Do not use the logo as part of your own product's icon or branding.

## Open Source and Trademark

The Desygn AI source code is licensed under the MIT License — you are free to use, copy, modify, and distribute the code under those terms. However, **MIT license rights do not extend to the Desygn AI name or logo**.

This means:

- You can fork the project and build your own product from the code.
- You must rename your fork or derivative product — do not call it "Desygn AI".
- Your fork must not use the Desygn AI logo without permission.

## Requesting Permission

If you would like to use the Desygn AI name or logo in a way not covered above, please open a GitHub issue or contact the maintainers directly. We are generally open to reasonable uses and will respond promptly.

## Reporting Misuse

If you believe someone is misusing the Desygn AI trademark, please notify the maintainers by opening a GitHub issue labeled `trademark`.
